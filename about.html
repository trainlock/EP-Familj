About Us

  • What is LegoFind?

    • LegoFind is the product of four students at LIU's work during the project stage in the course Electronic Publishing. Through this website, the user gets the possibility to search for a single piece in the LIU Lego database, either by its name or the id, and receive a list of sets wherein it is included.
