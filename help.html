How to search

Search for a lego piece by a part of its name or ID. Use the search bar on the upper right side of this page or go to the start page to find your desired lego piece.

Navigate in between results by using the next and previous buttons and then click on the desired piece to retreive information about, colour, part ID and which sets you can find it in.
